HOME EPISODES EVENTS ABOUT  ME

Hi, I'm Greystar.

I'm 19, a college student, and I make music.

    I started Braindance News as a passion project in 2018. Back then, it was a short segment on "Snare Up", an internet radio show. It evolved into a podcast. After highschool, I took a break.
    In college, I came back to the project and tried to give it new life doing monthly mixes, and using social media pages to share new music from underground artists.
    Brainrave was the first event I organized as Braindance News. It involved over 40 musicians and artists, and it was a GREAT success! It wouldn't have been possible without the help of others. I'd like to continue in this direction... more events! I love to make things happen.

I'm excited for the future of this project.
